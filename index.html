About

This site provides supplemental materials for Twinder, a prototype search engine for Twitter based on Twitter Analytical Platform (TAP). The work of TAP has been published as a full paper, titled "Facilitating Twitter Data Analytics: Platform, Language and Functionality", at IEEE International Conference on Big Data 2014. This research work is mainly conducted at Web Information Systems group at TU Delft.

Demonstration

Currently we are providing a live demonstration of Twinder at http://twinder.org.

Evaluations

Thanks very much for the comments that reviews have given for our IEEE Big Data 2014 paper. Due to the length limitation of the paper, we could not accomodate all the evaluation details in the paper. However, we provide all the detailed information here.
